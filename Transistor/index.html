Applications

The transistor is widely used in various applications, from radios and televisions to computers and mobile devices, revolutionizing the way we communicate and process information.

How It Works

The transistor operates by using semiconductor materials to control the flow of electrical current. It consists of three layers: the emitter, base, and collector, which work together to amplify or switch signals.
