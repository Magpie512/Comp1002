Enzo's Journey

From our home to yours

In 1952, Enzo Marinelli arrived in America with nothing but a worn recipe book, a heart full of dreams, and the authentic flavors of his beloved Naples. What started as a small corner shop has blossomed into the neighborhood gem you know and love today.

Enzo's son Giuseppe carried on the tradition, perfecting the art of hand-stretched dough and sourcing the finest San Marzano tomatoes directly from the volcanic soil of Mount Vesuvius. Today, Giuseppe's daughter Maria continues the family legacy, ensuring every pizza honors the authentic recipes passed down through generations.

The Marinelli Family: Enzo, Giuseppe, and Maria

Our Philosophy

Simplicity, Quality, and Passion

"La pizza non è solo cibo, è cultura" meaning "Pizza isn't just food, it's culture." This is what Nonna Marinelli used to say, and it's a philosophy we live by every single day. We believe that the best pizza comes from the heart, made with ingredients that tell a story, and shared with people who become family.

Every morning, we hand-select our ingredients, knead our dough with care, and fire up our ovens with the same passion that Enzo brought from the cobblestone streets of Naples over 70 years ago. When you taste our pizza, you're not just enjoying a meal, you're experiencing a piece of Italian heritage, lovingly preserved and proudly shared.
